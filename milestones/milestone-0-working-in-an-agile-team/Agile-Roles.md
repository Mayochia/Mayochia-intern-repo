# Research & Learn

## What are the key roles in an Agile team, and what does each role do?
- Developers. Build the product â€” including frontend, backend, and mobile engineers. They are the ones who technically implement a feature and responsible for the quality of code.
- QA (Quality Assurance). Collaborates with developers to ensure that the output is of quality, follows the requirement, and write tests.
- Product Manager (PM). Communicates with the customer  to help them understand the progress, challenges, and prioritization of the development. They ensure that the team's work aligns with the company's goals.
- Agile Project Manager (Agile PM). Supports the team by clearing what stops the team from working. They facilitate meetings and ensure that the team follows Agile principles.
- Other supporting roles (e.g., stakeholders, UX, customer support). Provide the strategic feedback, user-centric design, and real-world insights necessary to refine the product.

## How do these roles collaborate in Kanban vs. Scrum?
|Roles|Kanban|Scrum |
|:--|:--|:--|
|Developers|Pull tasks continuously based on capacity and sync with the team as blockers arise.|Commit to a set of tasks for sprint and collaborate during Daily Standups.|
|QA (Quality Assurance)|Test items as soon as they are ready to keep the flow moving |Focus on testing all Sprint items to ensure they meet the "Definition of Done" before the Sprint ends.|
|Product Manager (PM)|Refine the backlog in real-time and provide just-in-time priority updates for the next available "pull."|Prioritize the backlog before Sprint Planning and avoid changing requirements during the active Sprint.|
|Agile Project Manager (Agile PM)|Monitor the board for bottlenecks and facilitate "stop-the-line" huddles to resolve workflow issues.|Protect the team from outside distractions and facilitate structured ceremonies like Retrospectives.|
|Supporting roles|Provide feedback (Support) and design assets (UX) asynchronously or as needed per active ticket.|Participate in Sprint Reviews to provide feedback on the increment and plan for future Sprints.|
