# User Manual for May

### My role
---
-  I am working as a Mobile App developer. I do tasks related to this intern role.
- The days and times I work are: Monday to Friday, 9:00AM to 5:00PM (flexible, remote)

### How I Work Best
---
I do my best work when:
- I organized my tasks and deadlines.
- I know my goals and priorities.
- Workspace is organized and no distractions.
- Environment is not hot and not too cold.
- I have music on.

I struggle when:
- Unclear instructions
- No mentor to ask guidance
- No feedbacks
- Noisy environment
- Frequently interrupted

### Communication Style
---
 I prefer my messages to be direct for quick updates:
- If this is a task, it has to be detailed.
- If possible, message instead of call. But if it is a long and complex task, I prefer face-to-face or video call.

The best way to reach me is:
- Email
- Discord
- Direct PM on Messenger

If you need something urgent, do this:
- Call in Discord
- Phone call with contact details already recorded

### Collaboration & Teamwork
---
I work best in a team when:
- Everyone shares their idea/opinion/concern
- Eager to listen and vocal
- Open-minded
- On-time/punctual
- Respectful of each others differences and time
- Responsible and do work with full effort

My biggest pet peeve in teamwork is:
- Always has to be right and does not take accountability
- No respect of time to others
- Does not communicate and no progress updates
- Passive
- Does not work in every possible solution and relies too much on others

If I seem quiet in a meeting, it usually means:
- I do not feel well
- I am still digesting and understanding the discussion

### Focus & Productivity
---
I stay focused by:
- Setting my mind and body to finish my tasks of the day
- A structured to-do lists dissected based on its difficulty and priority
- No distractions

Things that distract me:
- Frequent interruptions in the middle of working hours
- Late notifications or changes of plans
- Too much noise
- Gossiping

The best way to support my productivity is:
- Detailed instructions and tasks
- Provide deadlines ahead
- No unncessary talk especially if not related to work

### Feedback & Growth
---
I like feedback to be:
- Direct
- Firm, but respectful
- Backsup with examples
- Has advice to help me navigate on how I should go about it

When I make a mistake, the best way to let me know is:
- Have a direct and calm tone

I appreciate it when teammates:
- Do not make me feel less if I make an oversight due to inexperience
- Calm when talking
- Not passive aggressive
